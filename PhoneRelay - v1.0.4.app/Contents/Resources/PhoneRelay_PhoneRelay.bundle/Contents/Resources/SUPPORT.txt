PhoneRelay Support

For support, include:

- PhoneRelay version and build from the About tab.
- macOS version.
- Android version.
- Connection type: USB, Wireless debugging, or legacy Wi-Fi adb.
- Whether USB mirroring works before Wi-Fi handoff.
- The selected device name and adb serial or Wi-Fi address.

If a connection or mirroring step fails, open the app log from the Help menu and include the relevant error lines when reporting the issue.

Useful diagnostics include whether Android USB debugging or Wireless debugging is enabled, whether Local Network permission is allowed on macOS, and the last action you took before the failure.
